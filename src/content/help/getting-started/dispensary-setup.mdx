# Dispensary Setup Guide

Set up your dispensary workspace so inventory, orders, customers, and AI tools all operate from the same source of truth.

## Setup goals

By the end of this guide, you should have:

- A working dispensary profile
- Menu or POS connectivity
- Order and inventory visibility
- Loyalty and CRM foundations
- At least one AI workflow ready to use

## Step 1: Confirm your dispensary profile

Start with the operational basics:

- Store name and location details
- Operating hours
- Pickup and delivery settings
- Contact information for staff and customers

This keeps public pages, order flows, and support surfaces aligned.

## Step 2: Connect your menu source

Choose the canonical menu source for your dispensary:

- POS integration if your system is already supported
- CSV import for an initial migration
- Manual setup only if your catalog is still very small

Verify after import:

- Product names and categories look right
- Inventory counts are reasonable
- Pricing and variants match reality
- Images and descriptions are customer-ready

## Step 3: Check order operations

Make sure the dashboard reflects how your store actually fulfills orders:

- Pickup workflow
- Delivery workflow
- Staff review and confirmation steps
- Notifications for new orders

If your store uses multiple fulfillment modes, test each one before launch.

## Step 4: Enable loyalty and customer visibility

A strong dispensary setup needs customer context, not just menu data.

Review:

- Loyalty settings and point logic
- Customer profiles and order history
- Segments or outreach lists
- Approval steps for customer-facing drafts

<Callout type="tip">
Even if you are not launching loyalty immediately, get the customer data path working early. It improves analytics, segmentation, and future retention campaigns.
</Callout>

## Step 5: Turn on one AI-assisted workflow

A good starting point is one of these:

- Smart upsells
- Approval queue review
- CRM intelligence
- Outreach draft generation
- Analytics dashboard review

Pick the workflow your team will actually use this week. The best setup is the one that gets exercised right away.

## Launch checklist

1. Inventory is visible and current
2. Orders appear in the dashboard
3. Core staff have correct roles and permissions
4. A help article or SOP exists for the team
5. One AI workflow has been tested end to end
