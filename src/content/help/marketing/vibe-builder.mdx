# Vibe Builder - Visual Website Builder

Build professional websites for your cannabis business without writing code. Vibe Builder is a drag-and-drop visual editor that turns your Vibe Studio themes into full, publishable websites.

## What is Vibe Builder?

Vibe Builder is BakedBot's visual website builder powered by GrapesJS. It lets you create, edit, and publish complete websites from your dashboard - no coding skills required.

**Perfect for:**
- Dispensaries needing a marketing website
- Brands launching campaign landing pages
- Associations building member portals
- Anyone who wants a professional web presence

<Callout type="tip">
**Start from a Template**: Browse our Template Marketplace for professionally designed starting points. One click to install and customize.
</Callout>

## Getting Started

### Access Vibe Builder

Navigate to **Dashboard → Marketing → Vibe Builder** or visit **bakedbot.ai/vibe/builder**

### Create a New Project

1. Click **"New Project"**
2. Choose a starting point:
   - **Blank Canvas** - Start from scratch
   - **From Template** - Browse the Template Marketplace
   - **From Vibe** - Convert a Vibe Studio theme into a website
3. Name your project
4. Start editing in the visual editor

### The Visual Editor

The editor has three main areas:

**Left Panel - Block Library**
Drag blocks onto your page:
- **Layout**: Sections, columns, containers
- **Content**: Text, images, videos, icons
- **Cannabis**: Product grids, menus, strain cards
- **Forms**: Contact forms, newsletter signup, age gates
- **E-commerce**: Cart, checkout buttons

**Center - Canvas**
Your live page preview. Click any element to select and edit it. Drag to reposition. Resize handles on corners and edges.

**Right Panel - Settings**
When you select an element, configure:
- Typography (font, size, weight, color)
- Spacing (margin, padding)
- Background (color, image, gradient)
- Border and shadows
- Responsive behavior

### Save and Auto-save

Your work saves automatically as you edit. You can also manually save with **Ctrl+S** or the Save button. All changes are versioned in your project history.

## Template Marketplace

Browse community-created templates at **bakedbot.ai/vibe/templates**.

### Browsing Templates

- **Search** by name or keyword
- **Filter** by category (e-commerce, landing page, portfolio, etc.)
- **Sort** by popularity, newest, or rating
- **Preview** any template before installing

### Installing a Template

1. Find a template you like
2. Click **"Use Template"**
3. Template is copied to your projects
4. Customize in the visual editor
5. Your changes don't affect the original template

<Callout type="info">
**Templates are starting points** - once installed, you own the copy and can change anything. The original template creator is not affected by your edits.
</Callout>

### Submitting Templates

Created something great? Share it with the community:

1. Open your project in the editor
2. Click **"Submit as Template"**
3. Add a description and category
4. Submit for review
5. Our team reviews and approves quality templates

## Publishing Your Website

### Publish to BakedBot Hosting

Every published site gets a free subdomain: **yourname.bakedbot.site**

1. Open your project
2. Click **"Publish"**
3. Choose a subdomain (e.g., `mycannabisbrand`)
4. Click **"Publish Website"**
5. Your site is live at `mycannabisbrand.bakedbot.site`

### Republishing

Made changes? Simply click **"Republish"** to update your live site instantly.

### Unpublishing

Need to take your site down? Click **"Unpublish"** to remove it from public access. Your project is preserved and can be republished anytime.

<Callout type="tip">
**Connect a Custom Domain**: Want to use your own domain like `www.yourbusiness.com`? See the Custom Domains guide for setup instructions.
</Callout>

## Tips for Great Websites

### Content Best Practices
- Keep your homepage focused on one clear call-to-action
- Use high-quality images (cannabis photography matters!)
- Include your dispensary hours and location prominently
- Add an age verification gate for compliance

### SEO Tips
- Set a descriptive page title and meta description
- Use heading hierarchy (H1, H2, H3)
- Add alt text to all images
- Connect a custom domain for best SEO results

### Mobile Optimization
- Preview your site on mobile using the device toggle
- Ensure text is readable without zooming
- Make buttons large enough for touch (44px minimum)
- Test forms on mobile devices
