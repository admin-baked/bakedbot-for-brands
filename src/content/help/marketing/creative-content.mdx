# Creative Center

Creative Center is the fastest path from blank page to a usable campaign draft inside BakedBot.

## What good onboarding looks like

Start here in order:

1. Finish your Brand Guide so Craig has your voice, colors, and guardrails.
2. Open Creative Center and generate one real draft for an upcoming promotion.
3. Tighten the hook, offer, and CTA instead of trying to perfect everything at once.
4. Move the winning draft onto your calendar so the workflow becomes operational.

## Best first use cases

- Weekly promo posts
- Product drop announcements
- Event recap or event teaser creative
- Store-level educational content
- Loyalty and repeat-visit reminders

## How Craig fits in

Craig uses your Brand Guide and workspace context to draft content faster, but the best results still come from a quick human pass:

- Confirm the offer is real
- Check compliance language
- Match the CTA to the channel
- Approve only what you are ready to publish

## Pair it with other onboarding steps

- Brand Guide makes outputs sharper
- Competitive Intelligence gives you better angles to write from
- Inbox becomes the place where drafts and approvals land
