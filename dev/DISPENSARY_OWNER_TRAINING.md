# BakedBot AI: Dispensary Owner Training Guide

Welcome to BakedBot AI, your Agentic Commerce OS for the cannabis industry. This platform is powered by specialized AI agents—Smokey, Craig, Pops, Ezal, Money Mike, Mrs. Parker, and Deebo—who act as your digital workforce.

This document serves as a comprehensive training manual detailing each feature of your dashboard, concluding with a "Week in the Life" simulation to help you seamlessly integrate these tools into your daily operations.

---

## 1. Workspace
Your daily operational hub and collaboration layer.

*   **Inbox:** A unified messaging interface switching between threaded conversations with your team and *Agent Chat*. Communicate directly with your AI agents to rapidly deploy tasks.
*   **Projects & Playbooks:** Automate your operations. Use **Playbooks** to enable workflows like the "Daily Intelligence Snapshot" via Ezal or "Low Stock Alerts" via Smokey. Create playbooks directly from natural language prompts.
*   **Drive:** The secure file management center to store and organize crucial dispensary assets and brand documents, accessible by your AI agents.
*   **Strategy (CEO Boardroom):** Executive-level control. Access the "Boardroom" tab to command operations, evaluate QA, run system health checks, and view morning briefings.

## 2. Pops (Analyst)
Your personal data scientist tracking the metrics that matter.

*   **Goals:** Define specific OKRs (Objectives and Key Results) across different timeframes and monitor your organization's real-time progress.
*   **Analytics:** A consolidated view of total revenue, average order value, sales by product/category, and daily stats. Identifies highest-performing categories and alerts you to churn.

## 3. Menu & Inventory
Your storefront OS, directly synced with your POS (e.g., Alleaves).

*   **Menu & Products:** Manage your synced catalog. Validate THC/CBD percentages and **crucially**, input Cost of Goods Sold (COGS) to ensure margin health and prevent over-discounting. Use the *Live Preview* tab to see exactly what customers see.
*   **Carousels, Hero Banners & Bundles:** Build dynamic, curated shopping experiences. Configure "Top Sellers" or "Staff Picks" carousels and create powerful bundle deals.
*   **Budtender (Ask Smokey):** Test your menu using the *Ask Smokey* tab. Interact with the chat exactly as your customers would to verify product recommendations and strain effects.

## 4. Orders
Managing fulfillment and your physical footprint.

*   **Delivery:** Manage delivery zones, track drivers in real-time with ETA calculations, and monitor NY OCM compliance to ensure fulfillment operations run smoothly.

## 5. Pricing
Maximize margins and optimize cart values.

*   **Smart Upsells [NEW]:** Powered by *Money Mike*. Leverage AI-driven product pairing strategies at checkout to intuitively increase your average order value (AOV) based on margin health.

## 6. Customers (Mrs. Parker)
Your loyalty and retention engine.

*   **Customers & Segments:** Build deep CRM profiles based on purchasing behavior. 
*   **Loyalty & Loyalty Settings:** Define your points engine, configure reward tiers (e.g., VIP, Gold, Platinum), and automate tier-advancement mechanics.
*   **QR Sign-up:** Generate unique, store-specific or campaign-specific QR codes to capture customer loyalty data instantly on the actual dispensary floor.

## 7. Marketing
Your digital growth engine, powered by *Craig*.

*   **Brand Guide & Brands We Carry:** Maintain a singular source of truth for your brand voice. Feature specific vendor brands across your domain.
*   **Creative Center & Vibe Studio:** Generate stunning, compliant imagery using *Creative Larry* (fal.ai FLUX.1 pipeline) tailored specifically for your brand aesthetics.
*   **Campaigns:** Command *Craig* to create multi-channel (SMS/Email) outreach programs. Seamlessly route campaigns through *Deebo* for age gate and language compliance checks before sending.

## 8. Intelligence (Ezal)
The automated "Lookout" for your competitive strategy.

*   **Market Intel:** Pulls data from CannMenus and other endpoints to provide weekly CI reports, tracking local competitor stock and pricing.
*   **GreenLedger [NEW]:** The single source of truth for auditing transactions, understanding financial routing, and gaining complete visibility into platform performance.

## 9. Admin
Back-office configuration and foundation.

*   **Settings & Custom Domains:** Configure your operator profile and link your custom vanity domain for your digital storefront.
*   **App Store:** Manage native integrations (e.g., SendGrid, Alpine IQ, POS systems).
*   **Team & Email Warm-up:** Invite team members (Budtenders, Managers). Utilize automated email warm-up to ensure *Craig's* marketing emails land in the primary inbox.

## 📅 The "Week in the Life" Simulation

*Simulating a Dispensary Owner's first week optimizing their business.*

### Day 1: Foundation & POS Sync
1.  Navigate to **Admin > Settings** to finalize your profile and verify your **Custom Domain**.
2.  Go to **Menu & Inventory > Menu** and hit "Sync with POS". 
3.  Click the **Products** tab and identify items with a "Not Set" badge under Cost of Goods Sold (COGS). Enter the cost data so your margin calculations lock in.
4.  Switch to the **Live Preview** tab to see your digital storefront.

### Day 2: Loyalty & Audience Building
1.  Open **Customers > Loyalty Settings**. Configure your points-per-dollar ratio and set up your VIP tiers.
2.  Navigate to **Customers > QR Sign-up**. Generate a QR code flyer and instruct staff to display it at checkout.
3.  Head to **Pricing > Smart Upsells**. Turn on AI pairing to increase cart sizes for new sign-ups.

### Day 3: Creative Setup & First Outreach
1.  Enter **Marketing > Brand Guide** to define your dispensary's unique tone (e.g., "Educated but casual"). 
2.  Use **Marketing > Creative Center** to generate two hero banners for the weekend push.
3.  Navigate to **Marketing > Campaigns**. Ask *Craig* to draft an SMS campaign to all customers who haven't visited in 30 days. Let *Deebo* run the compliance check, then schedule the send.

### Day 4: Playbooks & Automation
1.  Go to **Workspace > Playbooks**. 
2.  Enable the **Low Stock Alert** playbook so you get an automatic inbox ping when top movers fall below 10 units.
3.  Activate the **Daily Intelligence Snapshot** playbook to have *Ezal* deliver a morning report.

### Day 5: Assessing the Floor
1.  Use the **Workspace > Inbox** to answer any budtender questions or review automated alerts generated by your Playbooks.
2.  Open **Pops > Analytics** to review the performance of your recent campaign and track customer sign-ups from your QR codes.
3.  In **Menu > Budtender**, test *Smokey* by asking "What's a good strain for sleep under $40?" to ensure it recommends your high-margin products.

### Day 6: Fulfillment & Competitor Watch
1.  Head to **Orders > Delivery** to optimize delivery routes for the busy weekend and dispatch your drivers.
2.  Read the **Intelligence > Market Intel** report to confirm your pricing on 1g vape carts matches local competitors for the weekend rush.

### Day 7: Strategic Review
1.  Enter the **Workspace > Strategy** dashboard (CEO Boardroom). Review the top-level Morning Briefing.
2.  Access **Pops > Goals** to assess whether you hit your revenue OKR for the week, and confidently plan your next week alongside your AI team.
